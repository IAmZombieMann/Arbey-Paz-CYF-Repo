Essential Warzone Survival Tips

  • Contracts: Always pick up Bounties or Scavengers early to build your bank.
  • Loadouts: Getting your custom weapons is the single most important mid-game goal.
  • High Ground: Always prioritize the high ground during final rotations to maintain visibility.
  • Communication: Use pings effectively to highlight enemies and loot for your teammates.
